Mapping Drone & Missile Strikes
Hi! This tracker is a work-in-progress project by Adya Madhavan. You can use it to explore the drone and missile strikes that have taken place through the course of the war in West Asia, since the 28th of February.
Each event represents a reported strike incident, not an individual projectile. Locations are approximations based on news reports, not exact coordinates. This is an ongoing project and is not for operational use.
